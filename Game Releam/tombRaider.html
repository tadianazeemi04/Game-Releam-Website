Shadow of the Tomb Raider

Storyline

Shadow of the Tomb Raider continues the journey of Lara Croft as she ventures into the jungles of South America to stop a Mayan apocalypse.

Gameplay

The game features a mix of exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat, with a strong emphasis on stealth.

System Specifications

  • OS: Windows 7/8/10
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-3220
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 660/GTX 1050 or AMD Radeon HD 7770
