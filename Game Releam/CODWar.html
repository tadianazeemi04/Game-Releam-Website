Call of Duty: Warzone

Storyline

Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale game set in the Call of Duty universe, featuring large-scale battles in the fictional city of Verdansk.

Gameplay

The game features a mix of traditional Call of Duty gunplay with battle royale mechanics, including a unique respawn system.

System Specifications

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-4340 or AMD FX-6300
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 670 / GeForce GTX 1650 or Radeon HD 7950
